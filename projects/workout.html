Long Stretch

This should be your most important pre-workout ritual! Good range of motion and warm muscles help prevent injury. Always find time to incorporate this into your pre or post workout.

    Start with good deep breathing. In through the nose, out through the mouth slowly. Hold each stretch for 10 - 15 seconds.

    For added practice try to incoporate box breathing.

  • Hands above your head and reach back.
  • Feet together and come straight down to touch your toes.
  • Come back up then spread your feet apart, go down to your left, right, and middle now.
  • Standing straight up again pull your foot back behind you and oush your hips forward to stretch your quadricep. Left and right.
  • Find a table or chair to extend your leg on. With your leg raised reach out and touch your toes. Right then left.
  • Next move to the ground, butterfly stretch. Pull both feet into a diamond shape then pull them close to your hips.
  • From there extend both feet out, Put your right foot over your left leg then pull your knee to you chest. Right then left.
  • Flip over to a pushup position and put your right foot on top of your left ankle to stretch your calf.
  • Finally up on both feet find a wall to stretch your chest out. Rest your palm on the wall then with your elbow bent lean forward and push against the wall.
  • If you have any other tight muscles use this time to stretch them freely.


Day 1: Chest and Triceps

This day focuses on a "push" motion. Good form is important as well as hitting each part of the muscle groups! 10 - 12 reps for 3 sets.

    Always start with a long stretch... See above.

  • Exercise 1: Half Mile Jog
  • Exercise 2: Machine Bench Press
  • Exercise 3:Machine Pinches
  • Exercise 4: Elevated Dumbbell Bench Press
  • Exercise 5: Tricep Cable Rope Pulldowns
  • Exercise 6: Tricep Cable Flat Bar Pulldowns
  • Exercise 7:Pushups till Failure
  • Exercise 8: Planks for 30 - 45 - 60 seconds. Leave your arms fully extended for added challenge.

If you have the option, finish with a cold plunge and time in the sauna!
